Research Areas

Computational Immunology

  • Developed single-cell genomics and machine learning frameworks to distinguish activated T cell subsets with >95% accuracy using minimal gene sets.
  • Identified novel markers (e.g., TRIM) and key gene signatures of antigen-responsive CD8+ T cells.
  • Organized a TCR-epitope prediction benchmarking hackathon to evaluate the performance of different tools.
  • Established metabolic requirements for CD4+ T cell function under stress.

Comparative Genomics

  • Created scalable tools (CESAR, TOGA) for genome-wide orthology and gene annotation across hundreds of species.
  • Systematically identified gene losses linked to dietary and ecological adaptations.
  • Revealed molecular mechanisms of evolutionary adaptation in mammals through comparative genomics.
  • Developed tools to improve the utility of genome alignments for the purpose of comparative genome annotation.

Recoding and Frameshifting

  • Performed systematic analysis of non-canonical translation mechanisms in bacteria, identifying >6,000 genes utilizing programmed ribosomal frameshifting and transcriptional realignment.
  • Characterized frameshift motifs and efficiency rules for different frameshift-prone motifs.
  • Developed visualization tools (CodonLogo) for codon pattern analysis.
